West Coast Wild Flower Delights Overnight Tour

Travel from Cape Town and enjoy the charm and beauty of the spectacular wildflowers of the Cape West Coast. This overnight tour will take you through long stretches of colourful carpets of flowers and view the magnificent West Coast National Park and fossil collection of the region. Enjoy the coastal scenery of the coastal town of Langebaan from where the industrial iron ore marine village of Saldanha Bay is visible across the lagoon. Stay in four star accommodation on the Langebaan lagoon and enjoy the seafood feasts caught and prepared by local residents at a seaside restaurant. Enjoy a boat cruise across the Langebaan lagoon to Churchaven for picnic among the wildflowers amid spectacular rock formations and magnificent surroundings of white beaches and turquoise Atlantic Ocean lagoon areas. Travel along the Olifantsriver and experience this unique wine region and special offerings of the hospitable locals and farming communities. This tour that is tailor made to suit the needs of every individual, makes for a memorable West Coast experience of a lifetime.
