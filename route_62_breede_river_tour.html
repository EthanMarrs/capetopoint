Schedule

09h00 Depart Cape Town
10h00 Arrive Stellenbosch for a scenic drive through town past the University of Stellenbosch, and optional walk and tour of four oldest houses of the Cape.
10h45 Depart to Somerset West
11h00 Arrive Lourensford Wine Estate (1700) Enjoy a tasting of your choice, at this estate steeped in history. Find out more about the work of many generations who have produced superior wine, and more recently coffee roasting and tastings, a home bakery, delicatessen, all amid a spectacular mountain setting.
12h00 Depart Lourensford
12h15 Arrive Morgenster Wine Estate (1711) Explore one of South Africa’s most successful olive and olive oil producers of export quality and awards. Taste local cheeses, olives, varieties of extra virgin olive oil, olive pastes, and wine tasting. Morgenster has received international acclaim for its Bordeaux style wines. 
13h15 Depart to Vergelegen Estate (1685)
13h30 to 14h30 Lunch at Staples Restaurant and Bistro This restaurant setting forms a link between old and new architectural designs of the estate, from where you will enjoy magnificent views of the Helderberg and Schaapenberg mountains.
14h30 Enjoy the outdoor features of this estate. Join an optional garden tour to view the 1 000 year old Camphor Trees in this internationally acclaimed wine estate garden of 18 varieties, some of the oldest Yellow wood trees in the country, or the Cammillia Garden of Excellence, in full bloom during winter.
15h00 Attend an optional wine tasting, or an accompanied tour of the original homestead, slave lodge site, and library, or a special cellar tour by prior arrangement. Explore the estate’s unique, award winning vintages in the Flagship, Reserve and Premium ranges.
16h00 Return to Cape Town
