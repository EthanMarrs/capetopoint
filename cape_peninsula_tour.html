Cape Peninsula Full Day Tour

Enjoy the flexibility of this tour to create your own lasting impressions of the fairest Cape. Discover the natural beauty and cultural heritage in and around Cape Town’s Unesco World Heritage sites. Travel the circular route between the Indian and Atlantic Oceans, and experience the magnificence of Table Mountain National Park and the Cape Floral Kingdom in and around the peninsula, and around to Cape Point Reserve. Enjoy the spectacular scenery along the coast with views of Table Bay, Hout Bay, Chapmans Peak, Cape of Good Hope, Simon’s Bay, and False Bay. Find yourself in the company of a local Cape resident and tourist guide, who is passionate about sharing the natural riches, culture and history of the Cape Peninsula. Not only will you experience flexible departure and return times, but also a selection of sightseeing stops of your own choice. Relax, sit back and enjoy the magnificent scenery, cultural history, good food, wine and coffee culture.

City of Cape Town scenic tour of historic city sites, cultural walkabout on Parliament Avenue in the Company Gardens
Table Mountain National Park one - and a half hour cable car tour, photographic stops at Signal Hill - home to the Cape noon gun that sounds at 12h00 across the Northern most tip of the TMNP. Drive to Lions head for panoramic 360 degree views of Table Mountain, the city of Cape Town, Table Bay Harbour and the Atlantic Seaboard.
Robben Island optional three-hour return ocean cruise across Table Bay, guided museum tour of the maximum security prison where Nelson Mandela was incarcerated for 18 years, and a bus tour of the island Entry Fee: R300 per adult, R160 children under 18
Atlantic Coast drive past the Twelve Apostles mountain range and Llundudno Beach
Hout Bay two hour, Seal Island/Whale boat cruise depending on season Entry Fee: R80 per adult, R40 children under 14, free for children younger than two
Chapmans Peak Drive, optional stops at Cape Point Vineyards in Noordhoek, Kommetjie beach and Lighthouse, Scarborough beach, to Cape Point
Cape Point Nature Reserve - optional one hour, funicular trip/walk to Cape Point Lighthouse Entry Fee R125,00 per adult, R65,00 children under 18, Funicular return adults R58,00 children R24,00
Cape of Good Hope - optional drive or board walk – choice of a one way walk and collection at the Cape of Good Hope meeting point, or return board walk and collection at the main car park. Moderate fitness levels
Dias Beach – spectacular views from the lighthouse, and/or optional and additional stairway return walk to the public parking point. Moderate fitness levels
Penguin Colony Boulders Beach – View the home of the endangered African Penguin colony as you walk along three boardwalks with views of three pristine Indian Ocean beaches Entry Fee: Adults R60,00, R30,00 for children under 12
Simonstown - SA Naval base, False Bay Yacht Club, historic sites dating back to British occupation and apartheid eras, colonial architecture, optional heritage museum visit
False Bay Coast Kalkbay - famous cuisine, historic whale station and fishing harbour, St James, Muizenberg historic battle site
Constantia wine route – Optional estate choices for tasting/pairing, or cellar tour by appointment, top cuisine, old homesteads in Cape Dutch architecture, gardens, history, Cost: Wine tasting/Governeur chocolate pairing, cellar tour from R80
Kirstenbosch National Botanical Gardens – optional one-hour walking guided tour, wheelchair friendly free by prior arrangement Entry fee: Adults R55, students R30, Children six to 12 R15, children under six FREE, shuttle cart guided tour additional R65 per person
