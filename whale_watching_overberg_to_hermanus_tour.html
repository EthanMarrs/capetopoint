Whale Watching Overberg to Hermanus Overnight Tour

This overnight tour provides a rich overview of the magnificent coastal and natural scenery of the Overberg, Hermanus and Stanford regions. This scenic tour takes you along the famous Clarence Drive, most popular for the magnificent scenery past Gordons Bay from where you will have views of the False Bay coastline and Cape Point. Travel past the old fishing villages of Rooi Els and Pringle Bay past the pristine surroundings of the Kogelberg Nature Reserve before reaching the villages of Bettys Bay and Hermanus. Stop along the coast to watch penguins at Stony Point in Bettys Bay, and whales from the shore before departing on a scenic boat cruise to spot some of the Western Capeâ€™s most famous marine life. You may have views of any number of different whales, most frequently the Southern Right and Humpback Whales. Enjoy a delightful wine tasting along the picturesque Hemel en Aarde Wine Route, and authentic beer at the Birkenhead Breweries in the neighbouring village of Stanford. Enjoy the luxury of three of four star accommodation at a coastal boutique hotel or bed and breakfast establishment, all located within walking distance of the centre of Hermanus, the restaurants, and shopping area.
