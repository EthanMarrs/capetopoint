Ladies Full Day Pamper Tour

This delightful full day tour caters for those who seek a combined option for relaxation and beauty treatment combined with good coffee, bubbly, chocolate, and Cape cuisine. Enjoy a fun filled day in the company of friends or family for a tour with sumptuous variations designed to suit every taste and each individual’s needs. This tour provides time and flexibility to relax in the tranquillity of a local spa, to enjoy the local coffee and wine culture within reasonable travelling distances within the Southern Cape Peninsula. Your choice of activity and tour route for the day can be discuss and Select your choice of route and Visit a historic Cape Winelands estate for a cellar tour and chocolate and wine pairing, in true tradition. View the coastal outdoors from the luxury of an air conditioned vehicle, and find out more about the culture and heritage of the earliest residents of False Bay, who have lived in the villages of Kalkbay and Muizenberg for many generations. Enjoy the magnificent garden settings and historic buildings of the southern Cape wine estates and return along the scenic route of Chapmans Peak via Hout Bay. Make time to spoil yourself in the good company of friends or family. Make the best of a leisurely pairing of good and chocolate, as well as a sumptuous lunch cooked by locals, all while enjoying the scenic routes of the Southern Cape winelands.

Schedule

08h30 - 09h00 Depart from guesthouses to the historic village of Kalkbay and the Majestic Spa, where you will be introduced to the spa facilities, beauty treatments, and indoor relaxation areas.
09h00 Arrive for a one-hour beauty treatment of a neck and back massage plus a mini manicure - or - mini pedicure. Enjoy the alternate relaxation periods and exclusive use of the sauna and steam rooms when in a group of three to six, while others enjoy beauty therapy.
11h00 Enjoy and optional leisurely coffee walk along Kalkbay beach road. View the trendy art galleries, retail outlets, bakeries and restaurants, as well as the book store and classic Brass Bell restaurant on the beach.
11h30 Depart to Groot Constantia for a cellar tour and five wine Governeur’s chocolate pairing in the classic surroundings of this historic estate and old homestead.
13h00 Depart to a chef’s table of choice at a wine estate.
15h00 Return via Cape Point Vineyards for a wine tasting, coffee and spectacular views along the scenic Chapman’s Peak drive and Houtbay to the far South Cape Peninsula.
16h00 Travel along the Atlantic coastline of Kommetjie past Misty Cliffs and the ‘green’ village of Scarborough, and Redhill township. Stop at the top of Redhill for spectacular views of SA Naval Base and Simonstown Harbour. This is an ideal setting for scenic photography of the historic seaside village of Simonstown, from an elevated location.
16h30 Depart to guesthouses

Tour Note

This tour can be arranged to visit a spa and wine estate in Tokai, such as Steenberg Estate, with visits along the Constantia wine route, such as Groot Constantia for a Governeur’s wine and chococlate pairing, a tasting at Constantia Uitsig or Constantia Glen, and return along the scenic route via Hout Bay and Chapmans Peak.
